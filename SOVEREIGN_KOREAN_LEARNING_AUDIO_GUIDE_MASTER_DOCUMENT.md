## 🎙️ PRODUCTION GUIDELINES

### Technical Specifications

**Audio Quality Standards:**
- Sample Rate: 44.1kHz or 48kHz
- Bit Depth: 16-bit or 24-bit  
- Format: WAV (master) → MP3 (distribution)
- Volume Level: Target -6dB to -3dB maximum
- Voice Style: Warm, clear, confident

**Recording Setup:**
- Quiet recording environment
- Consistent microphone distance (6-8 inches)
- Natural, conversational pacing
- Clear Korean pronunciation
- Minimal background noise

### Production Timeline

**Week 1-2:** Essential Foundations & Arrival
**Week 3-4:** Daily Scenarios & Self-Intimacy  
**Week 5-6:** Transportation, Shopping, Social
**Week 7-8:** Emergency, Health, Tech, Cultural
**Week 9-10:** Quality control and final editing
